The Connection Retreat

A private gathering.
A privately-held place.

The venue is chosen for one thing: its ability to hold the work.

The Connection Retreat takes place in a privately-hired property — selected not for its proximity to an airport, but for its quality of stillness. It might be a farmhouse in the hills, a secluded estate, or a villa on the edge of something vast and quiet.

We look for places where the environment itself is doing something before a single session begins. Where nature, solitude, and beauty are already working on you. Where you can exhale on arrival and not pick the breath back up until you leave.

It is not a hotel retreat. It is a private gathering, held in a setting designed to disappear into — and re-emerge from changed.

What's included
  • 5–7 nights in a privately-hired venue
  • All meals, prepared daily
  • All workshops, sessions, and ceremonies
  • 10–16 guests — kept deliberately small
  • Pre-retreat preparation call with a facilitator
  • Post-retreat integration support
  • Arrival and farewell ceremonies
