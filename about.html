The Origin

This retreat didn't begin
as a business idea.

It began as a feeling — the sense that something essential is missing from how most of us move through the world. Not a problem to be solved, but a quality of aliveness that can be returned to, when the right conditions are held.

Flo and Maria met through the world of yoga and acroyoga — practices that, at their best, create exactly this: real contact, real presence, real trust. What began as a shared curiosity about what a retreat built around genuine connection would look like has become The Connection Retreat.

Not a product. A container — designed with care, held with warmth, and built to create something that lasts beyond the final session.

A dream. Lingering in the background for so long.

A space. Where compassion, inspiration and purpose come together.

A connection. So deep and unique that it asks to be shared.

Me. Driven to create a container.

You. Born to ground those inside of it.

Us. The heart and soul of The Connection Retreat.

Life. It's calling to be lived in unique ways.

Love. Forever at the centre of it.

Maria

Co-Facilitator · Yoga Teacher · Embodiment Guide

Maria brings to this work years of training and practice in yoga, acroyoga, and tantra — alongside a quality of presence that is difficult to name but immediately felt. She has the rare capacity to hold space for people at their edges with both warmth and precision.

Her facilitation is characterised by a deep attunement to the people in the room — a sensitivity to what is needed, and a willingness to meet it honestly. She doesn't perform presence. She embodies it.

Flo

Co-Founder · SOMA Breathwork · Thai Massage · Retreat Designer

Flo has organised and facilitated retreats internationally and approaches this work from a belief that the architecture of a shared experience matters — that what happens in a group can be shaped by how carefully the container is built.

His training spans SOMA breathwork, yoga, acroyoga, tantra, and Thai massage, and his facilitation is characterised by an attention to the unseen dynamics of a group — what is moving underneath the surface, what wants to be invited in.

He wrote the poem that gave The Connection Retreat its name. He is driven by a question that has stayed with him for years: what becomes possible when people feel truly safe with each other?

How We Work

What we believe
about this work

I

Safety before everything

Nothing real happens without felt safety. We build it carefully, slowly, and with consent at the centre of every practice. Participation in anything is always an invitation, never an expectation.

II

The body as the site of change

Insight that stays only in the mind tends not to last. We work with the body because that's where experience actually lives — and where lasting shifts actually happen.

III

Structure that serves freedom

The five-element framework isn't a programme — it's a container. What happens inside it emerges from the people who are there. Every retreat is unique because every group of humans is unique.

IV

Honest over impressive

We don't perform depth. We don't promise transformation. We create conditions, hold them well, and trust that what wants to happen — happens. Results that last come from what is real, not what is choreographed.
